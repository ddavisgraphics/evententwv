About Us

Founded in 2015 in Clarksburg, WV. After searching for party rentals for their own wedding in 2013 and not being able to find affordable options, the owners decided to open their own business serving customers looking for party rentals on a budget . EvenTent caters to but not limited to weddings, birthday parties, graduations, festivals, family reunions, tailgates, and sporting events. EvenTent, family owned, has started small but envisions growing quickly. We encourage you to check out our inventory, or give us a call and we will try our best to get what you need to make your party everything you envision.

Tent Facts

Table Information

Tables and tablecloths can really help to establish a mood, or fit with a specific theme of an event or party. It is recommended for informal dinners to use an 8” - 12” drop, formal dinners use a 10“ - 15” drop or laplenth, and lavish events use a 30” or floor length. Buffet, Dessert, and Gift tables should always use the 30” drop tablecloths.
